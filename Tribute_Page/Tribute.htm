Randy Rhoads

Guitar legend gone too soon

Randy Rhoads performing live in concert in 1981.
  • Randall (Randy) William Rhoads was born December 6th, 1956.
  • He was an infuential heavy metal guitarist who played with Quiet Riot and Ozzy Osbourne.
  • Died in a tragic plane crash while on tour in Florida on the morning of Friday, March 19th.
